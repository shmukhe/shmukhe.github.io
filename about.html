About Me

I'm a Junior at Georgia Tech graduating in May, 2027 with a BS in Computer Engineering. I chose to study computer engineering because of my experience in competitive highschool robotics, where I was able to enable world class autonomous software through building robust and consistent hardware. I want to continue enabling great software through hardware as a Computer Engineer.

I’m from a suburb of Phoenix, Arizona called Chandler and I love the Southwest (the sunsets, people, and food are unmatched). In highschool, I was involved in robotics competitions, both FRC and VEX Robotics and my school's cross country team. I still like to run, work out, and hike (especially when traveling) in my free time.

At Georgia Tech, I'm a Digtal Designer at the Silicon jackets Chip Design club to get practical experience in the chip bring up process from concept to silicon. I’ve also joined the Electrical and Computer Engineering Makerspace (the HIVE) as a Peer Instructor to continue tinkering.
