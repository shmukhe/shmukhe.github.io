About Me

I'm a Sophmore at Georgia Tech graduating in May, 2026 with a BS in Computer Engineering. I chose to study computer engineering because of my experience in competitive highschool robotics, where I was able to enable world class autonomous software through building robust and consistent hardware. I want to continue enabling great software through hardware through computer engineering.

I’m from a suburb of Phoenix, Arizona called Chandler and I love the Southwest (the sunsets, people, and food are unmatched). In highschool I was extremely involved in robotics competitions, both FRC and VEX Robotics and my school's cross country team. I still like to run, work out, and hike (especially when traveling) in my free time. I also enjoy exploring Anime and different TV shows with my friends.

At Georgia Tech, I've joined the Solar Racing team to immerse myself in a competitive, application-oriented environment reminiscent of the ones I thrived in during high school. I’ve also joined the Electrical and Computer Engineering Makerspace (the HIVE) as a Peer Instructor to continue tinkering.
